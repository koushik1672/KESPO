Payment Help
How to Pay with UPI
  1. Enter the payment amount and click "Generate QR Code"
  2. Open any UPI app on your phone
  3. Tap on "Scan QR Code" in your UPI app
  4. Scan the QR code displayed on the screen
  5. Verify the payment details and complete the transaction
  6. Click "I've Paid" to notify us about your payment
Payment Security
  • Your payment is processed securely via UPI
  • We do not store your UPI PIN or banking details
  • All transactions are encrypted and secure
Common Questions

Payment verification typically takes 5-10 minutes during business hours. If it takes longer, please contact our support team.

If you've paid the wrong amount, please contact our support team immediately with the transaction details. We'll help resolve the issue as soon as possible.

Once your payment is verified, you'll receive a confirmation email with the payment receipt. You can also download it from your payment history.
